What is Pollution?

Pollution, derived from the Latin word "polluere" meaning "to make dirty," refers to the introduction of harmful substances or activities into the environment, leading to a degradation of its quality and a disruption of natural processes.
Types of Pollution:
  • Air Pollution
  • Water Pollution
  • Soil Pollution etc..
